Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow yout taste buds festive focacci appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become yout new favorite starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

featured in:
fresh cranberry recipes that go beyond sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon istant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mizing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I somtimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
